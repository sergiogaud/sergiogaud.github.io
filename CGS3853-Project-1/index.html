About

Nintendo has undeniably been one of the most influential gaming companies in history and a household name. Founded in 1889 in Kyoto, Japan, the company initially focused on the production and distribution of Japanese playing cards. Little did they know that they would later become one of the world's largest video game companies, thanks to groundbreaking console releases like the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES), Game Boy, and Nintendo Switch. Nintendo has also produced some of the most iconic and beloved games in history, including Super Mario Bros., The Legend of Zelda, and Pok√©mon, all of which have left an lasting mark on the gaming industry.
